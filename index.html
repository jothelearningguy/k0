Why Lagos Needs This Program

Addressing the unique challenges facing Lagos entrepreneurs

Lack of Platform & Audience

The average aspiring founder in Lagos doesn't have a built-in platform or audience to launch from, making it harder to gain initial traction.

Limited Support Systems

Many founders rely solely on YouTube videos and content from Western creators, which often don't address the specific challenges of building in the Nigerian market.

Age Gap in Entrepreneurship

The existing entrepreneurial community skews older, with teens and young adults rarely given opportunities to start companies.

Missing Youth Ecosystem

There's no established ecosystem specifically designed to support young founders in Lagos.

Network Barriers

Without existing connections, it's nearly impossible for a Lagos founder to get their startup off the ground‚Äîthis program breaks down that barrier.

We Focus on the Person, Not the Idea

The idea can pivot, but the founder's drive, creativity, and execution ability are what matter most. We want to back the 19-year-old with a crazy vision over the polished pitch deck.

Focus on Social Impact: Building for Lagos

We prioritize social entrepreneurship and community impact. We're not looking for founders trying to replicate Silicon Valley success stories‚Äîwe want builders solving real problems for real Lagosians.

Lagos-First Thinking

Build solutions that directly address challenges faced by Lagos communities‚Äîtransportation, healthcare access, education gaps, financial inclusion, waste management, housing, or local commerce.

Measurable Community Impact

Your startup should create tangible, positive change in the lives of everyday Lagosians, not just serve the elite minority.

Sustainable Social Models

We support businesses that can be profitable while doing good‚Äîsocial impact and business success aren't mutually exclusive.

Local Ecosystem Growth

Preference for startups that create jobs, empower local entrepreneurs, or strengthen Lagos's economic fabric.

Building Resilience: The Reality Check

This residency isn't just about comfort and community‚Äîit's about testing your limits and showing you what it truly takes to build a successful startup.

We believe resilience is forged through challenge, not comfort. Through structured challenges, we're helping you discover what you're truly capable of.

The Residency Challenge Structure

Intense Build Weeks

Periodic "sprint weeks" where founders work on aggressive deadlines to ship features, validate assumptions, or hit growth milestones

Weekly Demo Days

Every Friday, present your progress to the cohort and mentors‚Äîaccountability builds momentum

48-Hour Hackathons

Quarterly challenges where you build something completely new from scratch, pushing creative and technical boundaries

Early Morning Sessions

Optional 6 AM founder circles for those serious about maximizing their day and building discipline

Customer Validation Challenges

Weekly targets for customer conversations, user signups, or revenue milestones‚Äîno hiding behind your laptop
