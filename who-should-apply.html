Is This For You?

We're looking for ambitious founders and creators who see the world differently and aren't afraid to build something radical.

We Focus on the Person, Not the Idea

The idea can pivot, but the founder's drive, creativity, and execution ability are what matter most.

We want to back the 19-year-old with a crazy vision over the polished pitch deck. We're looking for radical thinkers who see opportunities where others see obstacles, and who have the courage to build something that matters.

✓ Radical thinkers who see the world differently and challenge conventional wisdom
✓ Builders who execute and ship quickly—we value action over perfection
✓ Ambitious individuals willing to take unconventional paths and bet on themselves
✓ Founders with strong conviction and resilience—the ability to keep going when it gets hard
✓ People who think beyond conventional career trajectories and traditional paths

Ideal Candidates

If you identify with any of these, this residency is for you

Early-Stage Founders

You're building a tech company in the early stages—maybe you have an MVP, maybe you're still validating. You're past the "just an idea" phase and ready to build seriously.

  • Have a product or service in development
  • Ready to commit 3 months to focused building
  • Open to feedback and iteration
  • Willing to pivot if needed

African Market Builders

You're an entrepreneur interested in the African market, whether you're from Nigeria, the diaspora, or anywhere else. You see the opportunity and want to build here.

  • Passionate about solving African problems
  • Interested in Lagos/Nigerian market
  • Ready to understand local context
  • Committed to building for real users

Community Seekers

You're a builder looking for community and focused development time. You know that building alone is hard, and you want to be surrounded by other ambitious founders.

  • Value peer support and accountability
  • Want to learn from other founders
  • Ready to contribute to a community
  • Open to collaboration

Lagos Ecosystem Explorers

You're a founder who wants to experience Lagos's vibrant startup ecosystem firsthand. You want to understand the market, meet the players, and build connections.

  • Curious about Lagos tech scene
  • Ready to immerse in local culture
  • Want to build local connections
  • Open to new experiences

Social Impact Builders

You're building something that creates real, measurable impact for Lagos communities. You're not trying to replicate Silicon Valley—you're solving real problems for real people.

  • Focused on social impact
  • Building for Lagos communities
  • Measurable positive change
  • Sustainable business model

Creators & Artists

You're an artist, musician, writer, filmmaker, or designer building something meaningful. You see the connection between creativity and entrepreneurship.

  • Creating impactful art or culture
  • Building a creative business
  • Want to monetize your craft
  • Ready to grow your audience

What We're NOT Looking For

To help you decide if this is the right fit

Polished Pitch Decks Without Execution

We care more about what you've built than what you've pitched. Show us action, not just ideas.

Copycat Ideas

We're not interested in "Uber for X" or "Airbnb for Y" concepts. We want original thinking that addresses real Lagos problems.

People Looking for a Vacation

This is an intensive program. If you're looking for a 3-month break, this isn't it. We're building companies, not taking it easy.

Those Unwilling to Pivot

Startups require flexibility. If you're married to your first idea and unwilling to adapt, you might struggle here.

Elite-Only Solutions

We prioritize social impact. If your startup only serves the wealthy minority, it's probably not aligned with our mission.

Lone Wolves

This is a community program. If you're not interested in collaborating, sharing, or learning from others, this might not be for you.

Age & Background

We're specifically focused on young founders who are often overlooked

The existing entrepreneurial community in Lagos skews older, with teens and young adults rarely given opportunities to start companies. We're changing that.

No Age Limit, But...

While we don't have a strict age limit, we're particularly excited about founders in their late teens, twenties, and early thirties who are often overlooked by traditional programs and investors.

University Students Welcome

We actively recruit from Lagos universities (UNILAG, LASU, YABATECH, and others). If you're a student with a radical idea and the drive to build, we want to hear from you.

First-Time Founders Encouraged

You don't need previous startup experience. In fact, we love first-time founders who bring fresh perspectives and aren't constrained by "how things are done."

Diverse Backgrounds

We welcome founders from all backgrounds—tech, arts, sciences, business, or anywhere else. What matters is your drive and vision, not your degree or previous experience.

Application Process

Simple, straightforward, and focused on you

1

Submit Application

Tell us about yourself, what you're building, and why you want to join. We care more about your story than your pitch deck.

2

Initial Review

Our team reviews applications looking for drive, creativity, and execution ability. We're evaluating the person, not just the idea.

3

Interview

Selected candidates have a conversation with our team. This is a chance for us to get to know you and for you to ask questions.

4

Selection

We select a small, curated cohort of founders who will push each other to build something exceptional. You'll be notified of the decision.
